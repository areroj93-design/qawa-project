About Qawa Investment Ltd

Dual Meat Supply Leader

Qawa Investment Ltd is Kenya's premier integrated meat supply company offering both premium beef and quality goat meat. We source directly from established ranches, process meat at licensed facilities, and deliver to major retailers across Kenya while exporting to regional markets.

Our dual-product strategy with 100 bulls/week capacity plus complementary goat meat supply makes us the unique choice for complete meat supply solutions.

With operations spanning sourcing, processing, quality control, and logistics, we maintain complete control over our supply chain to ensure consistent excellence.
